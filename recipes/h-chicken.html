Hunters Chicken

Other Recipes

Description

Chicken breast, wrapped in bacon, smothered in tangy barbecue sauce and topped with melted cheese. A British pub classic that you can easily make at home for a quick weeknight dinner. No pans are needed - we cook it all right in the baking dish. Can be served with Chips or Mashed Potatos

  • Prep time:

    5 Mins

  • Cooking time:

    30 Mins

  • Serves:

    4

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 8 rashers/strips streaky bacon - smoked or unsmoked â€“ your preference
  • 240 ml BBQ sauce - Use good quality store-bought, or my quick homemade BBQ sauce (I used homemade)
  • 50 g grated/shredded mature cheddar cheese
  • 50 g grated/shredded mozzarella cheese
    • To serve:
  • chips or mashed potatoes
  • veggies - such as carrots, green beans or broccoli

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F (fan).
  2. Wrap each chicken breast in two rashers/strips of bacon and place in a shallow baking dish.
  3. Place in the oven and cook for 25 minutes U+2013 until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the middle.
  4. Remove the dish with the chicken from the oven. Spoon the BBQ sauce on and around the chicken.
  5. Sprinkle the chicken with the cheddar and mozzarella.
  6. lace back in the oven for 5 minutes, until the cheese has melted.
  7. erve the chicken with mashed potatoes/Chips and vegetables.
